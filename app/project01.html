Nature Photography

Living in the beautiful Lancaster County, nature photography has always been important to me. Being able to capture the radiant aesthetic of a rare natural landscape has become an essential core from which all my other photography extends. Regularly hiking the various trails near my home is a quiet, thoughtful hobby which, I hope, is reflected in some of my favorite works.

Seasonal

I try to hike year-round. The changing of the seasons inspires hikers to view the same landscape from a different perspective; to celebrate the abundant vernal beauty of spring, the sparse harshness of winter, the cornucopia of color of the fall, and the celebration of light in summer.
