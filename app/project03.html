Street Photography

Portaiture has always been the scariest aspect of photography for me. There is always an increased sense of pressure to capture some ethereal, transcendental aspect of a person solely through a photograph. Those who have a talent for this always thoroughly impress me. While this may not be my strength it is a skill I wish to develop further. I am always want to capture the indefinable aspect of a person, an emotion, in an instant.

Portraits

When shooting people I try to focus less on actively shaping them to pose in a certain way. My goal is always to capture a person in a moment in time. This technique allows them not only to approve of how they might look in the photograph but also allows them to be reminded of a memory or emotion from that moment the memory was captured. It can be capturing lightning in a bottle.
